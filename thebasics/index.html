Dogs

Dogs are mammals, which were domesticated from wolves. The scientific name of dog is Canis lupus familiaris, which is similar to the wolf scientific name. Dogs are often referred to fondly as Man's Best Friend. Also, labradors (pictured above) are the number one dog in the world. Dogs are playful, cute pets, and everyone should have an opportunity to have one.
